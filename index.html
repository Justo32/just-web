Migration Pathway

The united kingdom has delibrately made relocation of medical personnel easy by the visa policy which is point based as similar to that of canada. Any professional who can get to that point is issued visa in 2 weeks which is a journey in getting a permanent resident in the U.K after 4 years.
This policy is causing a huge brain drain in third world countries, like Nigeria due to the salaries paid in foreign currencies and the table below showed the statistical data of nurses who immigrated quaterly during 2021.
